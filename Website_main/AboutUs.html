Our Story

*

La Dolce Vita was born from a passion for authentic Italian flavors and a dream of bringing them to the vibrant city of Mumbai. Established in 2018, La Dolce Vita was founded by chef Kenny and his partners Vedu and Convoy , who both shared a deep love for Italian culture, cuisine, and community. Nestled in the heart of Mumbai, La Dolce Vita combines classic Italian recipes with the rich spices and traditions of India, creating a one-of-a-kind dining experience.

Our Mission

*

Our mission is simple: to create a space where the essence of Italy is celebrated, right here in Mumbai. We believe that food should be a joyful experience, filled with quality ingredients, passion, and creativity. Our team is dedicated to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients, and we proudly serve dishes that are prepared with respect to Italian culinary traditions, while embracing the flavors of India.
